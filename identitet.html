Typography

Two typefaces carry the entire system: DM Sans for headlines and reading copy, DM Mono for labels, navigation and interactive UI text. A light weight throughout keeps the tone calm and confident rather than loud.

Primary typeface

DM Sans

Used for all headlines, sub-headlines and body copy. Set almost exclusively in a light (300) weight for an airy, modern tone; medium (500) is reserved for the wordmark and rare emphasis.

Supporting typeface

DM Mono

Used for navigation, buttons, eyebrows, form labels and footer text — always uppercase with wide letter-spacing to read as system/UI text rather than editorial copy.
